HealthChecker defines the signature of the client-side LB channel health checking function. The implementation is expected to create a health checking RPC stream by calling newStream(), watch for the health status of serviceName, and report it's health back by calling setConnectivityState(). The health checking protocol is defined at: https://github.com/grpc/grpc/blob/master/doc/health-checking.md
type HealthChecker func(ctx context.Context, newStream func(string) (interface{}, error), setConnectivityState func(connectivity.State, error), serviceName string) error
