Automatic port allocation is broken with OpenSSH before 6.0. See also https://bugzilla.mindrot.org/show_bug.cgi?id=2017. In particular, OpenSSH 5.9 sends a channelOpenMsg with port number 0, rather than the actual port number. This means you can never open two different listeners with auto allocated ports. We work around this by trying explicit ports until we succeed.
