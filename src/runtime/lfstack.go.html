lfstack is the head of a lock-free stack. The zero value of lfstack is an empty list. This stack is intrusive. Nodes must embed lfnode as the first field. The stack does not keep GC-visible pointers to nodes, so the caller is responsible for ensuring the nodes are not garbage collected (typically by allocating them from manually-managed memory).
